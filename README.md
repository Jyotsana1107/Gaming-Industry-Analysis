***📊 GAMING INDUSTRY PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS***

**🚀 Project Overview**

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of the global gaming industry. The objective was to extract meaningful insights from raw sales data and identify key factors driving revenue across genres, platforms, publishers, and regions.
The analysis focuses on transforming raw data into actionable business insights through structured data processing, KPI definition, and interactive visualization.

**🎯 Objectives**
- Analyze global video game sales trends
- Identify top-performing genres, platforms, and publishers
- Understand regional revenue distribution
- Derive actionable business insights and recommendations

**🧰 Tools & Technologies**
- Excel – Data cleaning, preprocessing, pivot analysis
- Power BI – Dashboard creation & data visualization

**📂 Dataset**
Dataset contains global video game sales data across:
- Genres
- Platforms
- Publishers
- Regions
- States

**🔧 Data Preparation**
- Removed duplicates and null values
- Standardized column formats
- Validated data consistency
- Structured dataset for analysis

**📊 Key KPIs Defined**
- Total Global Sales
- Regional Sales
- Sales by Genre
- Sales by Platform
- Sales by Publisher

**📈 Dashboard Highlights**
The interactive dashboard provides:
- Genre-wise performance analysis
- Platform contribution to total sales
- Publisher dominance in the market
- Regional revenue distribution
- Trend analysis across years

**🔍 Key Insights**
- Action genre dominates overall revenue, driven by high engagement
- PS2 emerges as the top-performing platform due to long lifecycle and wide adoption
- Nintendo leads among publishers, indicating strong franchise-driven success
- North America contributes the highest share of global sales

**⚠️ Risks Identified**
- Declining trends in certain segments suggest potential market saturation
- High dependency on a few publishers increases competitive risk
- Platform lifecycle impacts long-term revenue sustainability

**💡 Recommendations**
- Focus on high-performing genres like Action and RPG for better ROI
- Target North American market for maximum revenue impact
- Leverage successful platforms and franchises for scalability
- Monitor declining trends to adapt to changing market preferences
